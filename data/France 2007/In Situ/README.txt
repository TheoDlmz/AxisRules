This dataset was collected in 2007 for the French presidential election. Researcher proposed voters to try alternative voting rules at the exit of the voting station in 3 French city: Illkirch, Louvigny and Cigné.

Reference:

BAUJARD Antoinette, IGERSHEIM Herrade, « Expérimentation du vote par note et du vote par approbation le 22 avril 2007. Premiers résultats », Revue économique, 2009/1 (Vol. 60), p. 189-201. DOI : 10.3917/reco.601.0189. URL : https://www.cairn.info/revue-economique-2009-1-page-189.htm

voters.csv contains the city (and the number of the voting station) where voters voted

Two alternatives voting methods were tested:
* votes/approval.csv: Approval
* votes/scores.csv: Score Voting (0,1,2)
